How It Works

In case you were wondering about how we make your voices reach the beneficiaries, here we have mentioned the 3-step Content-Recording-Distribution process that we follow.

Content

We first approach blind schools to take their requirement for the type of content needed for the students. These could be poems, short stories, academic etc and in English or any Indian language. We then scout for it online & offline and then upload it on the website for recording.

Recording

Apart from www.projectawaaz.org we also plan to organise #LendYourVoice recording sessions in schools, colleges and corporate offices. If you represent one, please get in touch with us at admin@projectawaaz.org to conduct a session in your establishment.

Distribution

We distribute these recordings on a monthly basis to our partner NGOs and blind schools after thorough moderation of the quality. We are also working to to make these recordings reach children from the underprivileged sections.

Our beneficiaries

If you represent a blind school/NGO/any other social organisation that could benefit from these audio books please get in touch with us at admin@projectawaaz.org
